Battle.net Account Setup

Follow each step at your own pace. Use Tab and Enter to navigate. All data stays on your device.

One click opens Battle.net signup and starts automation (install the userscript once on the Auto-Fill tab). Email verification, SMS codes, and CAPTCHA may still need you — AccessForge fills the wizard and pauses there. Shortcut: Alt+F

Step 1 of 8
  1. 1

    Prepare your email

    Use an email you can access. Gmail, Outlook, or any provider works. You'll need it for verification.

  2. 2

    Open the signup page

    Click Create Account Now above to open signup and auto-fill the wizard. Or use Open Battle.net Signup for manual entry.

  3. 3

    Enter country & date of birth

    Select your country from the dropdown. Enter your birth date — this sets account restrictions for younger players.

  4. 4

    Create your BattleTag

    Your BattleTag is your public name (e.g. PlayerName#1234). Pick something you'll remember. Numbers are added automatically if your name is taken.

  5. 5

    Set email & password

    Use a strong password (12+ characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols). Save it in your Info Vault below or a password manager.

  6. 6

    Verify your email

    Blizzard sends a verification link. Check your inbox and spam folder. Click the link to confirm your account.

  7. 7

    Add phone number (if asked)

    Some accounts require phone verification. Use a number you can receive texts on. Check the Resource Console tab for sites you've saved, or Blizzard's phone help link above.

  8. 8

    Install Overwatch & play

    Download the Battle.net app from blizzard.com/download, log in, and install Overwatch 2 (free to play).
